SEECHUR AGRO PRIVATE LIMITED

“Human By Chance, Farmer By Choice”

SEECHUR AGRO is a forward-looking startup pioneering sustainable vertical and hydroponic farming in India. Our seed-stage venture focuses on cultivating Lakadong Turmeric — a high-curcumin variety native to Meghalaya — under controlled polyhouse conditions in Tamil Nadu.

We are inviting like-minded investors and partners to join us in building a scalable, sustainable, and high-value agricultural ecosystem.

Our Vision

To be a leading agri-tech venture creating sustainable, high-value crop ecosystems with technology, trust, and transparency.

Our Mission

To sustainably cultivate Lakadong turmeric and high-value crops using hydroponics and controlled environment farming to deliver consistent returns to stakeholders and healthier produce to consumers.
