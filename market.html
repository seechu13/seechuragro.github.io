Competitive Edge

  • Capital efficiency: Restart leveraging existing polyhouse/greenhouse assets.
  • Premium mix: Hydroponic vegetables for cash-flow + high-curcumin Lakadong for value.
  • Traceability: Batch-level testing & compliance targeting modern trade and export buyers.
  • Speed to market: Microgreens revenue from Day-30 post-investment; vegetables stabilise by Month-6.

Policy & Government Support

Mission Lakadong (Meghalaya)

State mission to scale area, productivity & value addition for Lakadong turmeric; supports testing, FPOs and processing infrastructure.

NHB & AIF

NHB: eligible infra gets up to 50% capex subsidy (cold storage/logistics). AIF: low-interest funding with interest subvention.

APEDA & Spices Board

Export facilitation, quality norms, and market linkage programs for organic spices and fresh produce.

Summary

Seechur Agro is positioned to capture the fastest-growing niches of Indian agribusiness—traceable hydroponic vegetables and high-curcumin Lakadong turmeric—by pairing capital-efficient refurbishment with strong B2B channels and policy alignment.
