Why Lakadong Turmeric?

What is Lakadong?

Lakadong turmeric is a premium variety from Meghalaya, known for consistently high curcumin levels (often 7–12%), desirable colour, and strong flavour. This natural potency drives demand from nutraceuticals, wellness brands, exporters and premium food manufacturers.

Why it fits our model

  • Premium pricing: High curcumin commands better realisations versus common turmeric.
  • Traceability & quality: Controlled cultivation improves purity, residue compliance, and consistent colour/curcumin.
  • Value-addition runway: Grinding, sterilisation, capsules, and extracts for export-ready SKUs.
  • Risk balance: Complements our protected-vegetable cashflows with a high-value spice line.

Key context from Indian agriculture

Our Lakadong plan

We scale Lakadong turmeric on our Edayarpakkam land, phasing from revival of protected vegetables to turmeric acreage expansion (Year 3 onward). The focus is on certified seed stock, best-practice curing, and testing for curcumin and residues to meet domestic and export standards.
