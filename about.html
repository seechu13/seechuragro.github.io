About Seechur Agro

Seechur Agro Private Limited is a Chennai-based agri-tech venture dedicated to sustainable hydroponic farming and controlled-environment agriculture. Our Edayarpakkam project encompasses 13.5 acres of land, with 4 acres under polyhouse and 1.25 acres under greenhouse cultivation. From year 3 onward, an additional 6 acres will be utilized for Lakadong turmeric—a high-curcumin variety renowned for its therapeutic value.

Our focus is to integrate modern technology with natural farming principles, ensuring high productivity, resource efficiency, and environmental balance. The Edayarpakkam project serves as a model unit for future expansion of hydroponic and Lakadong turmeric cultivation across India.
