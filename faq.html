Frequently Asked Questions

What crops are proposed to be cultivated under the hydroponic setup?
English cucumber (core), microgreens (daily B2B), select leafy greens. In parallel, Lakadong turmeric via nursery-to-field program.
What is the current status post-cyclone?
The farm requires refurbishment (cladding, fertigation, benches, electricals). Investment will restore full operations in ~3 months.
When do revenues start?
Microgreens by ~Day 30; hydroponic vegetables stabilize by ~Month 6; first Lakadong cheque by ~Month 7.
Who are the target buyers?
Modern retail & quick commerce for vegetables; APEDA/Spices-aligned buyers for Lakadong turmeric.
How do you manage quality and traceability?
Protected cultivation, hygiene SOPs, batch testing, and traceability across nursery and dispatch.
What are the key risks and mitigations?
Climate (insurance, protected structures), market (portfolio & channels), operations (staggered cycles), capital (phased deployment).
