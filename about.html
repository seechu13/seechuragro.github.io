About Seechur Agro

We are a Chennai-based agritech venture focused on hydroponics and protected cultivation. The Edaiyarpakkam facility operated successfully before the cyclone; we are now refurbishing and scaling with investor support.

Before & After (Cyclone)

Before Cyclone — full operations with stable yields.
After Cyclone — refurbishment required (cladding, fertigation, benches, electricals).

Vision & Mission

Vision: Build a sustainable, technology-integrated agriculture enterprise producing premium vegetables and Lakadong turmeric.

Mission: Combine precision hydroponics with traditional crop excellence to ensure consistent yield, traceability, and investor-grade governance.
